In this section, we introduce the notion of Dehn-twist pointed stable curves type outer representations as a certain generalization of IPSC-type [cf. \cite[Definition 2.4, (i)]{NodNon}] and NN-type [cf. \cite[Definition 2.4, (iii)]{NodNon}] outer representations, and investigate their basic properties. We then investigate the natural outer representations of semi-graph of anabelioids associated to the strata defined in the previous section, and in particular verify that the outer representations of inertia groups are of Dehn-twist pointed stable curves type.
